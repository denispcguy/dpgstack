# AI

## Definition: Purpose of AI in Dpgstack
To create apps faster. To come up with new ideas. AI is a fast and ruthless virus. It can significantly contribute to growth, at the cost of introducing mutations. The whole dpg-powered project should belong to human mind, introducing small controlled doses of AI mutations, while keeping the control and fighting it off with human immune system.

## Properties: AI Limitations
- Needs human supervision
- Does not know of beauty and simplicity.

## Properties: Types of work AI can do
- AI Agent: Dirty work of merging, updating, maintaining. Blue collar jobs. Context heavy.
- AI Chatbot: Learning, experimenting with ideas with little to no context.

## Workflow: Working with AI Agent
1. Make sure the task is obvious.
2. Describe it in 1-2 sentences.
3. If it's 80% aligned with your expectations, clarify. If not, start over.

## Workflow: Working with AI Chat (Google AI mode)
1. Describe a problem in abstract
2. Zoom in on details, ask dumb questions.
